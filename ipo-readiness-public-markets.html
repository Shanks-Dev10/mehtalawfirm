Services

Who we are

Mehta Global Advisory runs an end-to-end go-public program—diagnose, prepare, list, and operate as a public company—so timelines, disclosures, and controls stand up to investor, exchange, and regulator scrutiny across jurisdictions

What we do

  • Readiness diagnostics: eligibility checks, group/structure simplification, cap-table and ESOP design, governance and committee setup, ICFR/SOX-lite control mapping, related-party and litigation review, tax position and cash-to-GAAP/IFRS bridging.​
  • Offer documentation: author business/MD&A, risk factors, use-of-proceeds, capital structure, and governance sections; align disclosure controls and verification procedures.​
  • Financial work streams: coordinate restated financials and comfort-letter timelines with auditors; standardize schedules, tie-outs, and data-room artifacts.​
  • Intermediaries & Route: help select/coordinate merchant bankers, broker-dealers, counsel, registrars/transfer agents, market makers; advise on venue/route and marketing rules
  • Execution: diligence Q&A, site visits, verifications, pricing/allocations mechanics, exchange and central-depository interfaces, closing logistics.​​
  • Post-listing: periodic/event-driven disclosures, insider lists and blackout windows, investor-relations and earnings cadence, continuing-obligations tracker, object-change/shareholder-approval pathways.​

How we work

We present venue/route option sets with pros/cons, a critical-path plan with owners and dates, and a compliance calendar. We run a controlled data room, redline discipline, and a single issues log across advisors, auditors, lenders, and exchanges—keeping one defensible narrative from drafting to closing and beyond.
