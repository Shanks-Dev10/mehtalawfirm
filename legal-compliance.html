Transactions & Corporate

Mehta Global Advisory converts strategy into enforceable deals with predictable timelines. We map objectives, counterparties, and jurisdictions, propose clear structure options, then drive documentation and closing to a bank, regulator, and exchange-ready standard.

Our work spans from equity and debt transactions (term sheets, share/facility agreements, security and trusteeship, inter-creditor and escrow mechanics), M&A and joint ventures (from design to definitive documents and disclosures), governance (board processes, cap-table hygiene, insider controls), and go-public programs (offer/listing documentation and continuing-obligation playbooks).

Because many transactions are cross-border, we align drafting with securities rules, central depository practices, foreign-investment and remittance regimes, and market expectations so cash flows, consents, and filings tell one consistent story

Regulatory & Continuing Obligations

We operate a jurisdiction-agnostic compliance program that keeps registrations, approvals, disclosures, and ongoing obligations on calendar—so growth never outruns governance. Starting with a diagnostic pass, we help set up trackers and evidence packs based on entity and geography, then maintain a cadence with your board and management

Scope includes corporate and markets filings, registry and depository interfaces, central-bank notifications and reports, insider-list and disclosure controls, and policy frameworks covering KYC/AML, sanctions, and data protection.

For issuers and regulated entities, we coordinate periodic and event-driven disclosures and prepare for reviews, audits, and inspections. When scrutiny arises, we craft responses, align the fact pattern and documentation, and coordinate appearances or local opinions through licensed professionals—preserving a single, defensible storyline.
