Services

Tax & Audit Readiness

With a focus on accuracy and efficiency, our audit and tax consulting services
aim to optimize financial processes and uphold fiscal responsibility

Tax

MGA provides end-to-end, cross-border tax support that blends planning, execution, and litigation management into one coherent program. We design tax-efficient operating and capital structures, address treaty access and repatriation, and anticipate permanent-establishment and transfer-pricing exposure so decisions hold up with auditors, lenders, investors, and regulators.

On the indirect side, we navigate VAT/GST, customs and duties, and withholding on services, royalties, and interest—coordinating filings and documentation across jurisdictions with a single cadence and calendar.

For transactions and public markets activity, we integrate the tax workstream with your bankers, lawyers, and reporting teams so equity raises, debt placements, listings, and post-listing obligations proceed on schedule.

Audit & Reviews

Our assurance practice prepares organizations for investor, lender, and regulator scrutiny and sustains that readiness quarter after quarter. Through licensed affiliates, we deliver statutory audits and reviews under IFRS, US GAAP, and local GAAP, while supporting consolidations and group reporting

Inside the business, we strengthen governance through internal audit, risk-based testing, and control frameworks (including SOX/ICFR), and we close gaps with practical remediation plans. For companies approaching transactions or listings, we run pre-audit diagnostics, organize data rooms, standardize schedules and tie-outs, and maintain a clear issues log so surprises don’t surface at signing.

Post-audit, we help management and the Audit Committee track findings to closure and embed durable processes that meet regulator and exchange expectations.
