Services

Governance and Risk Management

With a focus on proactive risks management, We help businesses anticipate,
evaluate, and address potential risks to safeguard their interests and assets.

Governance & Policy

MGA designs the governance spine, board and committee charters, delegation of authority, disclosure controls, and a disciplined meeting cadence. We embed insider-list/blackout protocols and a policy library (code of conduct, conflicts, data protection, whistleblower), plus continuing obligations workflows for issuers and regulated entities

We set up compliance calendars, evidence packs, and dashboarded board packs, train teams, and coordinate licensed local counsel where required.

Deliverables include charters, policies, calendars, board/committee packs, training records, and playbooks that withstand investor, lender, regulator, and exchange scrutiny.

Risk

MGA implements enterprise risk management that scales across borders, risk appetite statements, registers, control matrices, KRIs, and RCSAs tied to accountable owners

We integrate KYC/AML and sanctions, third-party/vendor risk, privacy and information-security baselines, and incident response with tested BCP/DR. For public or regulated companies, we align risk with disclosure controls and audit programs to drive findings to closure.

Outputs include risk registers, heat maps, testing results, incident runbooks, drill reports, and closure notes—giving management real-time visibility and an auditable trail.
